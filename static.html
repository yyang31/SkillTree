This activity is designed to support your career development. These are nine soft skills many advance professionals use in their careers. Please follow the instructions before engaging with the activity.

Instructions:

  1. Start at the center of the activity with the large circle and think about how to answer the question.
  2. The basic skills are closer to the circle. Advanced skills are at the outer edges of the activity, furthest from the center. Advanced skills require learning the basic skills first.
  3. Read through some of the basic skills that you think would benefit your career development. You may also read the advanced skills interest you in relation to your career goals.
  4. Count the number of skills you would be interested in developing and put that number in each of the text box at the top of the page.
  5. Allocate a total of 50 skill points to move on.
